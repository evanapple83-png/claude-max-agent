# Summarize

1. **Read the whole thing first.** Don't summarize from the opening paragraphs.
2. **Lead with the bottom line** — one sentence the reader could act on if they read nothing else.
3. **Then the key points**, in order of importance, not order of appearance. Use short bullets.
4. **Preserve meaning, not length.** Keep specifics that change a decision (numbers, names, dates, caveats); drop filler.
5. **Never invent.** If the source is ambiguous, say so rather than smoothing it over.
6. **Match the depth to the ask** — a one-line TL;DR, a paragraph, or a structured brief.

Default shape: a one-line summary, then 3-7 bullets, then any open questions or risks worth flagging.
