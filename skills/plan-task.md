# Plan task

1. **Restate the goal** in one sentence and name what "done" looks like — the verifiable success criteria.
2. **Surface unknowns first.** List assumptions and the questions whose answers would change the plan. Ask the blocking ones before planning further.
3. **Break it into ordered steps** — each step concrete, small enough to verify, and dependency-aware (what must come before what).
4. **Flag risks** — the steps most likely to fail or take longest, and a fallback for each.
5. **Right-size it.** A two-line task gets a two-line plan; a multi-day effort gets phases with a checkpoint after each.

Output: success criteria, open questions, the ordered steps, and the riskiest part. Don't start executing until the plan is agreed if the work is large or hard to reverse.
