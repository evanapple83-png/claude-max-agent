# Proofread

1. **Fix what's wrong first:** grammar, spelling, punctuation, agreement, broken sentences.
2. **Then improve clarity:** tighten wordy phrases, cut redundancy, fix ambiguous references, prefer active voice.
3. **Preserve the author's voice and intent.** Don't rewrite their style or inject your own. Don't change technical claims.
4. **Keep the register** the author chose (formal, casual, marketing) unless asked to shift it.
5. **No em-dashes** unless the author already uses them; prefer commas, colons, or periods.

Output the corrected text. If you made non-trivial changes (not just typos), add a short list of what you changed and why, so the author can accept or reject.
